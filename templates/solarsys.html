🌞 The Solar System 🌍

☀️ Sun

The Sun is the heart of our solar system, a giant ball of hot plasma that provides energy for life on Earth.

🪐 Mercury

The smallest and fastest planet, Mercury orbits closest to the Sun and has a rocky, cratered surface.

🌕 Venus

Known for its thick, toxic atmosphere and extreme temperatures, Venus is often called Earth's sister planet.

🌎 Earth

Our home planet, Earth is the only known world with life, oceans, and a breathable atmosphere.

🔴 Mars

Called the Red Planet, Mars is a dry, dusty world with giant volcanoes and potential for past life.

🪐 Jupiter

The largest planet, Jupiter is a gas giant known for its Great Red Spot — a massive storm bigger than Earth.

🌀 Saturn

Famous for its stunning rings, Saturn is a beautiful gas giant with dozens of moons.

🌀 Uranus

Uranus is unique for its sideways rotation and pale blue color due to methane in its atmosphere.

🌀 Neptune

The farthest planet, Neptune is a cold blue gas giant with strong winds and dark storms.

🌑 Pluto (Dwarf Planet)

Once considered the ninth planet, Pluto is a tiny icy world in the Kuiper Belt, far beyond Neptune.
